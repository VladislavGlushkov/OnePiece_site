History of creation

The One Piece manga was first published in July of 1997. It was serialized in the manga anthology magazine, Weekly Shōnen Jump.

The first four chapters were then collected and released in December 1997. An anime adaptation of the manga began airing in October 1999.

Live action Seiries

Live action One piece was released Agust 23, 2023

The live action crew consists of Iñaki Godoy as Luffy, Mackenyu Arata as Zoro, Emily Rudd as Nami, Jacob Romeo Gibson as Ussop and Taz Skylar as Sanji
